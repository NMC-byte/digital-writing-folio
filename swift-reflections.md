### What makes a work a piece of E-literature?
Pieces of literature are often written or typed, with the goal of telling a story or a motive. A subsection of literature may be considered E-literature. Some examples of E-literature may be [Gathering Clouds](https://luckysoap.com/thegatheringcloud/) or [Taroko Gorge](https://collection.eliterature.org/3/work.html?work=taroko-gorge)

These works incorporate their digital space and features to create interactive or visual pieces that traditional literature can't utilize.

### Of the key examples above, which did you like most and why?
I've largely enjoyed [Gathering Clouds](https://luckysoap.com/thegatheringcloud/) as it does well to elaborate on the main point it's trying to convey, that being the digital cloud and the environmental impacts that come with it. The textual material itself does a good job of hooking me, but many visual and interactive components make the piece truly fascinating. From the addenda hidden within red text, to elements being slowly revealed when a reader lingers on one page for too long, one may see it not only as E-literature, but perhaps a puzzle.

### What makes a work a piece of E-literature?
Pieces of literature are often written or typed writing, with the goal of telling a story or motive. A subsection of literature may be considered E-literature. Some examples of E-literature may be [Gathering Clouds](https://luckysoap.com/thegatheringcloud/) or [Taroko Gorge](https://collection.eliterature.org/3/work.html?work=taroko-gorge)

These works incorporates their digital space and it's features to create interactive or visual pieces that traditional literature can't utilize.
